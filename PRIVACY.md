# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 28, 2026**

Epoch ("the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the extension handles your data.

## 1. Data Collection
The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to the developer or any third-party servers. 

## 2. Local Storage
All information entered into the extension, such as your:
- Date of Birth
- Custom motivational messages
- Theme preferences (fonts, colors, etc.)

is stored strictly within your browser's local Chrome Sync Storage. This data is used solely to provide the extension's functionality and may be synchronized across your own devices where you are signed into Chrome. The extension has no access to this data.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## 4. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the Web Store listing.
